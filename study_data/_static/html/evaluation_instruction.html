Evaluation Instructions

Welcome to the evaluation process! Please follow the steps below to complete your evaluation:

  1. Decision Making:Please respond with either Yes or No to the question: "Would you like to purchase the stock?"
  2. Reason: Write down the reason for your choice in the free text box labeled "The reason of your choice".
  3. Evaluation Criteria: After making your decision, please evaluate the system in the following aspects:
    • Trust: How much do you trust the financial advisor? Answer from 1 to 100.
    • Satisfaction: How satisfied are you with the financial advisor? Answer from 1 to 100.
    • Knowledgeable: How knowledgeable do you feel after interacting with the financial advisor? Answer from 1 to 100.
    • Helpful: How helpful do you find the financial advisor? Answer from 1 to 100.
  4. After completing all the evaluation criteria, click the "Send: Evaluation" button to submit your responses.

Thank you for your participation in this evaluation process!
